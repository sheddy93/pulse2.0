## 🎓 LESSONS FROM COMPETITORS

### What Personio Did Right
- ✅ Started in Germany (same language market) before expanding
- ✅ Built compliance modules (compliance = switching costs)
- ✅ Invested heavily in enterprise sales team
- ✅ Went for strategic partnerships (large consultancies)

### What Factorial Did Right
- ✅ Free tier (viral growth driver)
- ✅ Transparent pricing (no surprises)
- ✅ Fast, iterative product development
- ✅ Strong founder story (Spanish founders = local credibility)

### What BambooHR Got Wrong
- ❌ Feature-poor (easy to disrupt)
- ❌ Legacy tech stack (hard to scale)
- ❌ Limited international expansion
- ❌ No messaging/collaboration features

### PulseHR Should Avoid
- ❌ Feature creep (focus on core HR)
- ❌ Complex pricing (keep it simple like BambooHR)
- ❌ Poor onboarding (invest in UX)
- ❌ No customer success (CAC > LTV)
